Comment OpenTelemetry peut transformer votre monitoring en unifiant vos logs/metrics/traces

Link to full Conference Talk

At the Devoxx France 2022 conference, I gave a talk titled How OpenTelemetry can transform your monitoring by unifying your logs/metrics/traces - in French.

In this talk, I our experience using OpenTelemetry at Ubisoft, and how it helped us to unify our monitoring data.

I shared how we use the OpenTelemetry Collector in a Kubernetes environment, and a unified platform based on Grafana Tempo, Loki and Prometheus.
